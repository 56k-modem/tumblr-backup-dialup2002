The IBM logo throughout the years.

My uncle worked at IBM. He didn’t seem to know a lot about computers, oddly enough; in fact, I’m not even sure what he did there.

He was a big fan of Monty Python. He would act out their sketches line by line, laughing all the time. A real chore if you don’t like Monty Python, or IBM employees.

Actually, now that I think about it, I think he was their accountant.
